Participants

Research

Tiling

We study coloring, invariants and algebraic properties of tiling.

Number Theory

Primality tests, factorization of integers and elliptic curves.

Arithmetic Geometry

Algebraic curves, Iwasawa theory and the group of rationals points on the curve.

Self-assembly

The method of self-assembly based on organic molecules.

Alternative Computation

Developing of a computational device for arithmetics using self-assembly.

Quasicristals

Quasicristals have an ordered but non-periodic structure. Initially they were created in a lab but recently they were found in nature.

Welcome to Algorithmic Tiling

This project explores connections between Tiling, Number Theory and their applications in Self-assembly and Computation.

We have eight universities that join together to study this exciting topic.

Specialists in Aperiodic Tiling, Arithmetic Geometry, Number Theory and Cryptography exchange their expertise and ideas.
